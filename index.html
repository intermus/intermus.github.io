Franklin Eventoff invented Force Sensing Resistor (FSR) technology in 1977 and patented it in 1980. His innovations have earned prestigious recognition including the California Governor's Award for Innovation (1986) and the International IR 100 Award for FSR development (1987).

In 1985, Franklin founded Interlink Electronics (NASDAQ: LINK), which became a leading supplier of FSR technology globally. The company was recently acquired, validating decades of innovation and market leadership. In 2001, he founded Sensitronics, which today supplies FSRs to leading musical instrument manufacturers worldwide and provides IMI's vertical manufacturing integration.

Franklin's FSR technology powers products across multiple industries‚Äîfrom consumer electronics to medical devices and aerospace applications. IMI represents nearly 50 years of continuous FSR refinement, combining Franklin's patented technology with vertical manufacturing integration.
